LEARN THE FEATURES

  • IXP
    IXP is the marking that denotes the level of protection against water, dust, etc..
  • BATTERY LIFE
    Most devices have an expected battery life based on Earbuds, Case and Total hours.
  • IN APP
    The apps unlocks customization features that are available within the earbuds.
  • ANC
    Active Noise Canceling uses mics and speakers to reduce surrounding noises.
  • MICS
    Allows you to answer clear calls from your smartphone or to use voice commands.
  • APTX
    Transmits over Bluetooth without damaging the quality of your audio.
  • Qi
    Is transferring energy from charger to receiver via electromagnetic induction.
  • SMART TOUCH
    It features touch-sensitive pads that can perform different functions.
